About Fine Living Interiors & Construction

Who we Are

Fine Living Interiors and Construction is an NCA certified, international award- winning interior design and construction firm, recognized for excellence in design innovation, quality execution, and impactful project delivery. We are a fully registered company operating in Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania, delivering residential, commercial, and institutional projects across the region. Our work reflects a deep understanding of design, construction, and cultural context, enabling us to create spaces that are both distinctive and functional. We pride ourselves on professionalism, originality, and the ability to manage projects holistically, from concept development and design to construction, finishing, and final handover.

Our Values

2. Quality & Craftsmanship

3. Affordability with Integrity
We are committed to delivering cost-effective solutions without compromising safety, durability, or design intent. Affordability is achieved through smart planning and responsible resource management.
4. People-Centered Design
We design and build with people at the core, settling families into homes and creating spaces that enhance comfort, dignity, and everyday living for all users.
5. Inclusivity & Universal Access
We believe great spaces must serve everyone. We intentionally consider persons who are differently abled in our designs, creating inclusive environments that support accessibility, independence, and belonging for all members of the family.

We actively promote diversity by training, mentoring, and empowering women within the construction and interiors industry, creating equitable opportunities for skills development and leadership.
7. Social & Economic Responsibility
We recognize our role in shaping communities and livelihoods. Through ethical practices, local sourcing, and skills development, we contribute to sustainable socio- economic growth.
8. Integrity & Professionalism

9. Sustainability & Long-Term Value
We design and construct with the future in mind ,prioritizing durability, responsible material use, and solutions that deliver lasting value over time.
